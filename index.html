Enterprise Design Sprints

--- by Richard Banfield

A Design Sprint provides a simple, time boxed problem-solving framework for product teams to get answers quickly and effectively. The exercises embedded in the five phases are designed to reduce politics, increase collaboration across functions and put the focus on answers (outcomes) and not just assets (outputs). Learn from enterprises like Home Depot, LearnVest, IBM, AARP, Fidelity Investments, Lidl, Blue Cross Blue Shield, and many more, how you can run, facilitate or sponsor your own design sprint and start delivering solutions in organizations of any size.
